WELCOME TO GREAT TURNING POINT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

At Great Turning point International School, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and inclusive environnment that fosters academic excellence, creativity and personal growth. Our mission is to empower students to become curious, confident, and compassionate individuals who are equipped to suceed in an ever-changing world.

As a community of learners we believe in the potential of every student to reach their full potential. Our experienced and passionate educators work tirelessly to create engaging and challenging learning experiences that cater to the diverse needs and interest of our students.

At Great Turning Point International School, we value:

  • Academic rigor and intellectual curiousity
  • Creativity, innovation and self expression
  • Emotional intelligence,empathy, and socia; responsibility
  • Diversity,equity and inclusion
  • Community engagement and partnership

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our school community,programs and values. We look forward to welcoming you to our family of learners and working together to shape the future of our students
